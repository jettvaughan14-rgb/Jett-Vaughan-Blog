About Me

My name is Jett Paul Vaughan. I am from a small town in East Texas named Mineola. It literally has 5 red lights and 1 Walmart. I was raised by my Grandparents on a private lake. After graduating from Mineola HS, I furthered my studies at Tyler Junior College, where I played saxophone for 2 years while studying and obtaining my AA in Advertising/PR. I plan to use this website as my Blog and my portfolio.
