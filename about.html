About Me

My name is Jett Paul Vaughan. I’m from a small town in East Texas right off Highway 80 called Mineola. After graduating from Mineola High School, I continued my education at Tyler Junior College, where I earned my Associate of Arts in Advertising and Public Relations. While at TJC, I was part of the TJC Band and played the baritone saxophone. In 2024, I was invited by my high school to perform with them at TMEA.

During my time in Tyler, I worked at Willow Brook Country Club as a server in their premier steakhouse, where I also helped create a few ads for the club. After completing my studies at TJC, I moved to Denton, Texas, to attend the University of North Texas, where I’m majoring in Advertising and minoring in Journalism.

With my degrees, I plan to work on political campaigns as part of a public relations team. I also aspire to join an advertising agency—whether it be Loomis or Miller Ad Agency, both based in Dallas, Texas. Eventually, I hope to earn my Master’s degree from either the University of Missouri or the University of Texas at Austin.

I plan to use this website to showcase my code and serve as a portfolio for my future endeavors.
